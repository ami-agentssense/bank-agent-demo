Loan offer rules

1. Ask in order:
   - loan amount
   - loan term (1 year, 5 years, or 10 years)
2. Use annual compound interest with rate 0.04:
   - Total Repayment = Principal × (1 + 0.04)^years
   - Monthly Payment = Total Repayment / (years × 12)
   - Total Interest   = Total Repayment - Principal
3. Present the offer and require confirmation:
   - "Would you like to proceed with this loan?"
4. Only submit the application after an explicit yes confirmation.
